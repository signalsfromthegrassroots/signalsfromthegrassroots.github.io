The 6th General Assembly of the World Youth Parliament for Water

The World Youth Parliament for Water (WYPW) and the International Secretariat for Water (ISW) are calling for applications for the 6th General Assembly, a pivotal event taking place within the framework of the 10th World Water Forum in Bali, Indonesia, in May 2024.

Objectives

Under the overarching theme “Water for Shared Prosperity,” participants will have the opportunity to:
  1. Learn from peers through interactive workshops.
  2. Explore Indonesian water issues and culture via field visits and activities with local communities.
  3. Engage with inspiring experts and fellow young water leaders.
  4. Influence the political process of the 10th World Water Forum.
  5. Present youth perspectives and priorities in various sessions and side events.
  6. Take actionable steps in their communities regarding vital water and climate issues.
  7. Contribute to shaping a robust and unified Global Youth Movement for Water.

Event Details:

  1. Date: May 14th – 24th, 2024
  2. Bali, Indonesia

Who Can Apply

Young individuals aged 18 to 35 worldwide passionate about water issues. Applicants need to be WYPW members, and if not, they can sign up through the application link below.

Selection Criteria:

The committee will consider:
  1. Involvement in the water or sanitation sector.
  2. Experience/background.
  3. Participation in existing WYPW chapters or working groups.
  4. Involvement in the 10th World Water Forum.
  5. Motivation and interest in the WYPW mission.
  6. Gender and geographical balance.

Cost and Financial Support:

Covered by Organizers
  1. Local transportation
  2. Accommodation
  3. Food
  4. Program expenses
  5. Participation fees to the World Water Forum
Covered by Participants
  1. International transportation
  2. Visa expenses
  3. Vaccines and other medications
  4. Insurance
