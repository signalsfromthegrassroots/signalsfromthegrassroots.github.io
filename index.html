Who We Are

Welcome to Signals from the Grassroots, a global youth-led NGO bridging the gap between young people's passions/aspirations and community needs

Lack of opportunities for youth is high, but there is certainly no lack of need in community. Signals from the Grassroots connects the dots; bridges the gap between young people’s passions/aspirations and community needs; by placing them where they are needed the most.

Our Vision

To be the hub of globally enlightened and dignified young men and women championing positive change

Our Approach

SftG's unique approach employs a four-pronged strategy:-

  1. I. We create projects that give young people the chance to actively engage in solving community problems.
  2. II. We provide youth with mentorship and financial support through The Signal Fund.
  3. III. We lobby and bring opportunities closer to young people to facilitate their community betterment efforts.
  4. IV. We amplify youth voices so that they are not only heard, but get to effect the course of events in society.

SftG stimulates youth to be action-oriented by not only developing their ability to formulate effective solutions to problems, but also providing them with financial support and mentorship.
In all our programs, Youth voices take front and center position and we work directly with young people to elevate their stories.
In our capacity strengthening of community systems, we highlight young people’s assets and emphasize the need to create safe spaces where young people learn from one another while developing healthy relationships with supportive adults.
Our mission is fulfilled when youth come to realize that there is enhancement in their abilities to control, influence or cope with their socio-economic roles in society.

The Signal Fund

At SftG , we firmly believe in the transformative power of youth-led initiatives in fostering positive change within communities. However, we are much aware that many remarkable youth efforts falter due to financial constraints.
Recognizing this challenge, we are proud to announce the launch of The Signal Fund.
The Signal Fund is dedicated to supporting youth-led efforts by providing trust-based funding, essential resources, and fostering youth-to-youth capacity development. Our aim is to empower young individuals or groups aged between 12 to 35 to drive impactful change in their communities.
Eligible applicants can apply for grants of up to 50,000 yen to support their initiatives, which can be utilized over a period of 12 months to sustain and scale their projects.
Apply for The Signal Fund and be the change you wish to see in your community.

Our Impact

1. In 2023, SftG embarked on a 12-month-long journey with our Dialogue for the Future Project. This project stimulated more than 90% of of our members to take on actions at an individual stage by establishing their own initiatives to solve the most pressing problems in their communities. They have further embarked on volunteering and internships to actively engage in bettering their communities. This highlights the tangible shifts in behaviors that SftG is facilitating among young people.

2. Our Keep Her In School With A Reusable Sanitary Pad Project will see the distribution of 2,000 pads and 1,000 antibacterial soaps to 1,000 school girls in 3 schools across 3 of Uganda's most rural districts of Mayuge, Kiboga and Rakai. It will enable these 1,000 schoolgirls to attend school consistently unburdened by menstrual concerns. The reusable nature of the 2 sanitary pads distributed to each student will sustain each girl for at least a year, alleviating the financial strain associated with disposable alternatives. The menstruation awareness sessions conducted at schools will greatly help in breaking the staunch stigma associated with menstruation among boys so that girls are not shamed of their periods.

3. Through use of media and storytelling, Signals from the Grassroots is enabling young people to share their stories and experiences, providing a counter-narrative that highlights their abilities and potential.
