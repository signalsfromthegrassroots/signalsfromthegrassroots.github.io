Briding the Gap

SftG believes that young people are the positive change that the world so badly needs

  • Signaling to change for the better
  • "A shared future"

The alarming rise in global youth unemployment highlights the wide disparities between young people and adults in the labour market.

Of Africa’s nearly 420 million youth, one-third are unemployed and another third is vulnerably employed! Youth face roughly double the unemployment rate as adults.

Our Approach

Signals from the Grassroots offers a multifaceted approach to connect young individuals with opportunities that align with their interests and skills. We collaborate with forward-thinking companies and organizations that understand the incredible potential that young individuals bring to the table. Our approach is versatile, offering both paid and unpaid opportunities to cater to the diverse aspirations and commitments of young people.

  1. Paid Opportunities
    1. Internships: We facilitate internships with companies and organizations that are willing to pay young talent for their services. This not only provides income for the youth but also empowers them to make a tangible impact on pressing issues.
    2. Temporary and Permanent Jobs: In addition to internships, we also connect young people to temporary or permanent job positions. This is particularly significant in the context of reducing long-term youth unemployment rates.

  2. UnPaid Opportunities
    1. Volunteering: Many young individuals are eager to volunteer their time and expertise to make a positive change in their communities. We offer unpaid opportunities for volunteering with organizations that are addressing critical issues.
    2. Internships for Experience: We connect young people with unpaid internships, where the focus is on skill development and experience. These opportunities allow youth to enhance their skills and gain valuable experience.
