SftG Mentoring Program

Youth-to-Youth capacity development

  • Signaling to change for the better
  • "A shared future"

At SftG , we firmly believe in the transformative power of youth-led solutions in bettering communities. However, many remarkable youth efforts falter due to lack of necessary knowledge and skills.
The SftG Mentoring Program provides every young person with the knowledge and skills they need to support their career ambitions. Through youth-to-youth capacity development, SftG is providing young people with insights into the world of business startups, financial literacy and work-readiness.
We deliver our mentoring 24/7 throughout the year. The program consists of a week of hour-long online webinars and panel discussions, hosted on Zoom and facilitated by our Mentoring team alongside volunteers.

Program Format

  1. Sessions will last around an hour and will be facilitated by The SftG Mentoring Programme Team and our professional volunteer mentors.
  2. Sessions will be delivered as Zoom webinars.
  3. The timetable for the week, with links to sign up for events, will be shared via email upon signing up.
  4. Sign-ups need to be done individually through the form below
